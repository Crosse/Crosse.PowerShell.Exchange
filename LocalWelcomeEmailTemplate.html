Welcome to JMU's Microsoft Exchange email system, the university's official means of electronic communication. Exchange provides faculty and staff with "anywhere access" to a system that integrates email, calendar, contacts and tasks. It also supports web access and a variety of desktop clients as well as mobile devices that utilize ActiveSync.

The Exchange email system provides:
  • a storage quota of 1.5 GB
  • an integrated Global Address List (GAL) that includes students, faculty, and staff
  • the ability to recover deleted items for up to 60 days
  • a consistent Microsoft Outlook experience across desktop and browser clients
